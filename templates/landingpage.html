What We Do

The GSU Monitoring System is dedicated to providing comprehensive inventory tracking and management solutions. We aim to streamline operations, optimize resources, and provide real-time insights through advanced monitoring and analytics.

Area Monitoring

Track completion status of different campus areas with visual progress indicators and real-time status updates for efficient facility management.

Inventory Tracking

Monitor stock levels across all departments with automated alerts for low inventory and comprehensive item usage tracking.

Borrowing System

Streamline equipment and resource borrowing with automated tracking, due date management, and return reminder systems.

Analytics & Reports

Gain valuable insights with comprehensive usage trends, performance metrics, and data-driven decision support tools.

Checklist Management

Create and manage custom checklists for maintenance, inspections, and routine tasks with visual completion tracking.

Admin Controls

Maintain complete oversight with comprehensive user permission management, request approvals, and system administration tools.

Real-time Alerts

Receive instant notifications for critical updates, low stock levels, overdue items, and system maintenance requirements.

Mobile Access

Access all monitoring features on-the-go with our responsive mobile interface designed for seamless touch interaction.
